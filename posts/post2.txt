"𝗔𝗿𝘁𝘀 𝗼𝗳 𝗕𝗲𝗻𝗴𝗮𝗹" is a photography series dedicated to capturing the diverse cultural heritage of Bangladesh. In this second chapter, KUETPS focuses on "Pohela Boishakh 1433", the vibrant celebration that marks the first day of the Bangla New Year. 

Experience the spirit of the new year through the local traditions that define this day. From the lively rallies and open-air concerts that define our celebration, to the excitement of the circus and the gathering of the people from all stages of life, these photos document the shared spirit and tradition of the people as we welcome the new year together.

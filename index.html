April 22, 2026

Arts of Bengal: Pohela Boishakh 1433

Arts of Bengal is a photography series dedicated to capturing the diverse cultural heritage of Bangladesh. In this second chapter, KUETPS focuses on Pohela Boishakh 1433, the vibrant celebration that marks the first day of the Bangla New Year.

Experience the spirit of the new year through the local traditions that define this day. From lively rallies and open-air concerts to the excitement of the circus and the gathering of people from all stages of life, these photos document the shared spirit and tradition as we welcome the new year together.
